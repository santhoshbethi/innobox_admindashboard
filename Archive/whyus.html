Process Details

Process Details

Our service offerings are way different from conventional service providers, we just donâ€™t provide solution basing on requirement. We work with our clients to realize their ideas into certified commercial products.

  • Ideation Discussion

    We meet our clients and understand the idea for which they are looking for a solution. Our team clearly listen to the clientâ€™s needs. With our experience we strengthen the idea with our inputs and get into discovery phase. Output of this particular phase is to get the idea either abstract or conceptual from client.

  • Discovery phase

    In this phase we bring back the client idea on to the table of experts. Our expert team starts brainstorming and analysing the idea. List out each and every technological needs. All the rudimentary technological needs are collected and documented. The technological needs can be either chipset selection, tools involved, front end possibilities, back end possibilities, middleware choices, memory needs, proprietary stack or open source, hardware module involved, power needs, performance targets, cloud needs, application possibilities, security aspects, types of standard involved, certification needs, compliance needs. This phase has two targets, first target being technologies involved and second target being preparation of architecture flow with different technological choices. We then discuss the architectures with our clients and indicate the pros and cons of each approach. Cost factor, complexity of use, availability and support of required hardware and software, interfaces involved are all focused in this phase. This phase will enable requirement phase for gathering and consolidating requirements

  • Requirement collection

    Using the inputs from discovery phase i.e. the finalized architecture flow diagram, each and every module is picked. The software, hardware expectations of each module, interfaces with neighbouring modules are carefully documented. We also identify the priority of each requirement. The requirements are segregated for each layer UI, middle ware and low level drivers. Performance expectations of each and every component is also captured as a requirement. This phase also captures high level and low level design requirements. We discuss these requirements with our clients each and every detail to ensure that expectations of the project based on requirements is clearly inline between both of us.

  • Milestone definitions

    A clear requirements in the previous phase helps in defining milestones that are needed for the project. Every milestone will be associated with some time lines. Each milestone is further divided into different sprints and all this is done in alignment with the clients. We understand the importance of costs involved in development of any product. So we ensure that wherever possible activities planned are developed in parallel for maximum productivity and efficiency. Our resource planning also happens in a very judicial way not to burden our clients.

  • Development

    In this phase, every activity planned in sprint is picked. Hardware development and software/firmware development happen in parallel. Our hardware engineers work on designing multi-layer boards to ensure. Multiple variants of boards will be planned namely development boards and reference boards. Before designing, the hardware modules needed and their placement on the board is thoroughly reviewed with our inhouse system experts. This phase takes care of important aspects like, minimizing interference issues on the board and also ensures easy bus connectivity between hardware blocks. Our software designers and developers define the software architecture to take care of inter processor communication, they customize the board support package of different SOC providers. A well-defined IoT platform API and network standards will be used throughout the development phase. A cross alignment between hardware and software architects happen regularly to ensure the needs of software are taken care in hardware development. This approach helps in no post silicon surprises. Unit testing is all done by respective software and hardware engineers.

  • Cloud Computing

    In this phase, the cloud computing needs of our client are evaluated. Our cloud architects understands the requirements and then propose the right backend service IaaS, PaaS, SaaS and right deployment models. We work on frontend requirements and develop a mobile application to access the backend services. Data security and privacy requirements are also analysed as per the client requirements. Our teamâ€™s proficiency in working with various cloud service providers like MS Azure, GC, AWS helped to build cloud based solutions to our clients for various products in a more shorter time.

  • Hardware Software Integration

    An integration team receives the hardware and software building blocks from different teams. All the software blocks are integrated as per the architecture needs. Integrated software/firmware is tested by stubbing the code at various levels. An application to verify the integrated software is developed. Overall firmware is built and flashed on to the hardware and functionalities are checked. Critical aspects of memory leaks, code compliance issues, open source violations are checked and fixed in this phase.

  • Verification and Validation

    Our stringent testing process will unearth any system issues in this phase. Different scenarios like jitters, interference measurements, loopback tests, speed tests, API conformance tests, inter processor communication effectiveness, command handling, processing delay. A test strategy capturing integration tests and system testing will be started almost in parallel to development. Test scripts needed to realize the test strategy are developed. Traceability between requirements and test case in test specification document are checked. This will help to fill if any gaps are there in the requirements. This approach helps that no requirement misses testing. All the issues observed in this phase will be reported back to our development team using bug tracking tools. Both pre-silicon i.e. FPGA level verification and post silicon validation are performed.

  • Compliance and Certification

    Different products need different types of compliances and certifications basing on their usage. A product that is needed in automotive will be different from product in consumer electronics, a product that is needed in health industry will be different from a product in retail. A product that is needed in industry or enterprise will be different from the product used in home. In designing these networking and IoT products basing on their end usage, there are multiple aspects that are considered, low power, light weight, less delay, no external noise, time sensitivity, response time, durability, ruggedness, precision, lesser false detections, local reforms of a country, carrier frequency allocations, different operator specifications, different SOC manufacture specification, carrier network expectations, different sensors expectations, weather conditions i.e. climate insensitivity, communication across multiple type of devices make and made, cloud platform needs etc.. All these differences lead to different standards and certification needs. Our expertise in compliance and certification helped in delivering multiple products.
