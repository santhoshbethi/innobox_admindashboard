Privacy Policy

Innobox is committed to respecting your online privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you share with us. The purpose of the online privacy policy (“Policy”), as amended from time to time, is to give you an understanding on how we intend to collect and use the Personal Information you provide to us. By accessing the website and providing us such Personal Information, you hereby consent to the collection and use of such Personal Information for the purposes disclosed in this Policy as well as those explained where Innobox collects Personal Information on the Web.

Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address, an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home and credit/debit card information.

Innobox has established this Policy so that you can understand the care with which we intend to treat your Personal Information. The following site discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices - www.innobox.com ("Site").

Use of the Information

The information request forms on our site require users to give us contact information (like their name and email address etc). We may use the contact information from the information request form to send you information about our company. Also, if you choose to provide us with your Personal Information, we may transfer the Personal Information among ourselves, our affiliates, subsidiaries, agents or to our third party service providers, across borders, and from your country and jurisdiction to other countries and jurisdictions across the world. By providing your Personal Information to us, you give us your consent to send you information about our company and products and for transfer of your information as stated above.

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Innobox does not utilize "cookies" and does not track any clickstream activities. The tracking technologies may however record information such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; and dates and times that our site is accessed. We may also analyze information that does not contain Personal Information for trends and statistics..

Children's Privacy

Innobox is not structured to attract children. Accordingly, we do not intend to collect Personal Information from anyone we know to be under 13 years of age.

This site may also contain links to other sites. Innobox is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites. You are advised to check the privacy policies of those sites before providing your personal information to them.

Security of Information

We are committed to protecting the security of your Personal Information. However, we may disclose Personal Information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to (a) conform to order any law or comply with any legal notice, process or order served on our website; (b) protect and defend our rights or property; or (c) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of our employees or agents, users of our products or services, or members of the public.
